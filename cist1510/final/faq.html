Frequently Asked Questions

Why is Web Design Important?

Website developers help make the internet easier to navigate and thereby help spread information. With this information, people can make more informed decisions alongside spread their ideas to help the world.

Designers have been raking in more and more money on average and with the rise of more information technology companies, this trend is bound to continue.

Should I learn Website Development?

Nearly everyone in the computer programming should get some basics in website development as often times backend languages such as Java and Python interact with the frontend and especially languages such as PHP and JavaScript.

How do I get started?

For many, it's often best to simply analyze other websites. GitHub provides a variety of open source examples which are made to be read by other developers and future developers. You can begin by simply modifying existing works or using snippets of them in your own and learn on your way with an objective on what to create.

For others, it may be ideal to enroll in a course designed to slowly expose materials such through your local technical college or an online platform supplemented by Devify.
